# Competitive Researching and CRO Strategy

## When to use this skill
- When starting a new project or redesign and needing to understand the competitive landscape.
- When the user mentions "competitor analysis", "market research", or "CRO strategy".
- When designing a landing page and needing inspiration from successful patterns in a specific niche.

## Workflow
- [ ] Identify 5 key competitors (local and international) in the given niche.
- [ ] Perform heuristic analysis for each competitor (Positioning, Conversion Architecture, UX/UI, Content, Social Proof).
- [ ] Create a differentiation matrix to identify gaps and opportunities.
- [ ] Generate an actionable Market Gaps Report with design and communication recommendations.

## Instructions

### 1. Market Context Discovery
Extract or ask for:
- **Company Name**: The business being analyzed.
- **Niche/Sector**: The industry vertical.
- **Value Proposition/Context**: Current standing, goals, and unique selling points.

### 2. Heuristic Analysis Framework
For each competitor, analyze:
- **Positioning**: What is their core promise? Who is their ideal customer?
- **Conversion Architecture**: 
  - Analyze CTAs (Copy, placement, color contrast).
  - Review lead capture forms (Length, fields, friction).
- **UX/UI & Vibe**: 
  - Color palette and emotional impact.
  - Typography and hierarchy.
  - Visual style (Minimalist, corporate, playful, etc.).
- **Content Strategy**: Tone of voice and recurring keywords.
- **Social Proof**: How do they build trust? (Studies, logos, testimonials).

### 3. Output Format
Results must be presented in:
- **Comparative Tables** for technical specs (CTAs, Forms, Page Speed if possible).
- **Competitor Deep-Dives** with observations and direct quotes from their copy.
- **Actionable Report**: A "Market Gaps" section highlighting what competitors are missing and how the subject can differentiate.
