# Example Analysis: CleanEnergy Latam

- **Company**: CleanEnergy Latam
- **Niche**: Industrial Solar Panel Installation
- **Context**: 10 years in Chile, expanding to Colombia. Focus: cost savings for factories.

## Competitor: SunPower Colombia
- **Positioning**: "Maximum efficiency for large-scale industry."
- **Conversion Architecture**: 
  - CTA: "Get a Free Energy Audit" (High-contrast yellow on dark blue).
  - Form: 4 steps, progressive disclosure.
- **UX/UI**: Professional, industrial look. High-quality drone footage of factory roofs.
- **Market Gap**: SunPower focuses on technology; CleanEnergy can focus on *10 years of cross-border experience* and *localized tax benefits in Colombia*.
